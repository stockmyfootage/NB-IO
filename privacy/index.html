privacy policy

Compliance: DPDP Act (India) | Updated May 2026

1. Data Fiduciary Identity

narrabrain.io is operated by Vibhav Nigam (Founder, Narrabrain Labs — Incorporation in Progress). For the purposes of the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, we act as the Data Fiduciary for information collected on this platform.

2. Information Collection & Purpose

We collect professional identifiers (email) and creative narrative briefs. This data is processed solely to:

  • Provide pilot access and blueprint credentials.
  • Refine our Narrative Delta Reinforcement Logic (NDRL) through user-provided corrections.
  • Facilitate high-intent referral funnels to partner cinematic libraries.

3. Data Localization & Sovereignty

In accordance with sovereign data mandates, all personal data and narrative inputs are processed and stored on Google Cloud Platform (GCP) Mumbai (Asia-South1). We do not transfer personal data outside the borders of India unless explicitly required for partner API fulfillment.

4. AI Transparency & Rights

Our Shared Contextual Logic Core (SCLC) interprets creative intent but does not "persist" proprietary briefs beyond the session's optimization needs. Users maintain the right to request erasure or correction of their professional identifiers at any time via info@narrabrain.io.
